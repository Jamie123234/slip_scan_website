Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use bank-level 256-bit encryption to protect your data. Your receipts and financial information are stored securely with redundant backups. We're GDPR compliant and SOC 2 certified.

What file types are supported?

Slip-Scan supports all standard image formats including JPG, PNG, HEIC, WEBP, as well as PDF documents. You can scan receipts directly with your phone camera or upload existing files.

Our AI achieves 99.9% accuracy in extracting vendor names, dates, totals, and line items. It intelligently detects tax amounts, tips, and discounts.
